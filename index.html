Hello! I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at UCLA Anderson School of Management, where I am advised by Paola Giuliano (chair), Michela Giorcelli, Nico Voigtländer, and Romain Wacziarg. I hold an M.A. in Computational Social Science with a concentration in Economics from the University of Chicago and a Bachelor of Economics and Finance from the University of Hong Kong.

My research focuses on the economics of science, economic history, and political economy.
